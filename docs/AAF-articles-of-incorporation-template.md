Form 4001 – Articles of Incorporation
Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act
1. Corporate Name
AlphaProMega Air Foundation
2. The province or territory in Canada where the registered office is situated
Ontario
3. The classes of members, if any
The Corporation has no members.
4. The number of directors
Minimum number: 1
Maximum number: 13
5. Statement of the purposes of the corporation
The purposes of the Corporation are exclusively non-profit and charitable, and include:
a. To advance education by developing and freely disseminating open-source knowledge, tools, frameworks, and educational resources in the fields of artificial general intelligence, zero-emission technologies, sustainable aviation systems, and ethical technological progress.
b. To advance science and research for the benefit of the public by conducting and supporting non-profit scientific inquiry into safe, valence-gated artificial general intelligence architectures, post-scarcity energy systems, biomimetic engineering, biosignature detection and preservation protocols for celestial bodies, and self-replicating robotic systems for sustainable resource utilization in space.
c. To carry out such other exclusively charitable purposes as are recognized by the Canada Revenue Agency from time to time.
6. Statement that the corporation is to carry on activities exclusively in furtherance of the above purposes
The Corporation shall carry on its activities exclusively in furtherance of the purposes set out in Article 5.
7. Statement regarding distribution of property on dissolution
Upon the dissolution of the Corporation, any remaining property shall be distributed to one or more qualified donees within the meaning of subsection 149.1(1) of the Income Tax Act (Canada), as determined by the directors at the time of dissolution.
8. Additional provisions, if any
a. The Corporation shall be carried on without the purpose of gain for its directors or members, and any profits or other accretions to the Corporation shall be used in promoting its purposes.
b. The Corporation shall not distribute any of its income or property to any director, officer, or member except to the extent permitted by the Act.
c. The Corporation shall comply with all applicable laws, including the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act and the Income Tax Act (Canada).
